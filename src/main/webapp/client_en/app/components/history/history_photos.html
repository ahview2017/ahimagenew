Haikou's premiere of the large modern folk dance "Gan Gong bird"

  • On the evening of April 12th, a large modern folk drama "Gan Gong bird" premiered in Hainan, Haikou. It is understood that Gan Gong bird is a legend of Hainan Li on love, spread widely in the local.
